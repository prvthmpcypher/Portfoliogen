Privacy Policy

PortfolioGen is designed around local browser use. The builder does not require accounts, dashboards, or server-side profile storage.

  • Portfolio details are entered and processed in the browser.
  • Exported ZIP files belong to the user.
  • Contact and newsletter forms are demo interfaces until connected to a form provider.
Privacy document

Terms of Service

Users may create, customize, download, and deploy portfolios generated with PortfolioGen for personal or professional use.

  • Do not use the product to host unlawful, misleading, or harmful content.
  • Generated content should be reviewed before publication.
  • Cypher Labs may improve templates, copy, and product behavior over time.
Service terms

Community Guidelines

PortfolioGen is intended for constructive professional identity, learning, and creative presentation.

  • Respect other people, brands, identities, and intellectual property.
  • Avoid harassment, impersonation, spam, and deceptive claims.
  • Report security, abuse, or content concerns through the contact page.
Community standard

Cookie Notice

The static site uses local browser storage for theme preferences and may load third-party assets such as fonts or icon stylesheets.

  • Dark and light mode preferences are stored locally.
  • No advertising cookies are intentionally set by the core static pages.
  • Third-party services may apply their own policies when external links are opened.
Cookie notice

Security and Reporting

If you notice a bug, broken export, unsafe behavior, or suspicious third-party dependency, contact Cypher Labs with reproduction steps.

  • Include browser, device, and page details where possible.
  • Do not submit secrets or private credentials through demo forms.
  • Responsible disclosure is appreciated for security issues.
Security contact

License and Ownership

Users keep ownership of the portfolio content they create and the exported website files they download.

  • Portfolio copy, project details, and images remain the user's responsibility.
  • Cypher Labs branding and product assets should not be misrepresented.
  • Generated templates may be modified after export.
